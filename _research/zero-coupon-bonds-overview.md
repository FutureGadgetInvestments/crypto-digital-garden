An overview of zero coupon bonds in DeFi and fixed income opportunities.

## What are Zero Coupon Bonds?

Zero coupon bonds are fixed income instruments sold at a discount to face value. The holder receives the full face value at maturity, with the difference representing the yield.

## DeFi Protocols

- **Pendle**: Yield tokenization and trading
- **Notional Finance**: Fixed rate lending
- **Element Finance**: Principal/yield token separation

## Key Considerations

- **Yield Sources**: Fixed rates, yield trading, arbitrage
- **Risks**: Smart contract risk, liquidity risk, interest rate risk
- **Strategies**: Buy and hold, yield trading, arbitrage
