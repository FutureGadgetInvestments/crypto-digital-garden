An overview of stablecoin types, mechanisms, and investment opportunities.

## Types of Stablecoins

- **Fiat-backed**: USDC, USDT, BUSD
- **Crypto-collateralized**: DAI, FRAX
- **Algorithmic**: Various designs with different risk profiles

## Key Considerations

- **Yield Opportunities**: Lending, LP positions, staking
- **Risks**: Depeg risk, regulatory risk, smart contract risk
- **Factors to Evaluate**: Backing transparency, audit history, market cap
